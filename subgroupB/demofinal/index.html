Who We Are

Together with our university team, we are documenting and analyzing carbon emissions data from office buildings across Singapore. Our mission is to help reduce emissions and contribute to a more sustainable future through data-driven insights and solutions.

How to help

Small changes in office operations can make a big difference. Join us to learn how your office can reduce its carbon footprint through data-informed decisions. We offer insights and actionable steps to minimize environmental impact.

What We Do

To address the increasing carbon emissions in Singapore's office buildings, we are conducting research using local data. We aim to provide accurate calculations and practical recommendations for office managers and building owners to reduce their carbon impact.

Where We Work

Based in Singapore, our research is focused on local office environments. We collect and analyze data from office buildings across the city to provide insights specifically tailored to Singapore's unique climate and energy consumption patterns.
